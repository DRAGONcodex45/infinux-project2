Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Infinux Web3 Development Company collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you visit our website, www.infinuxweb.com, and use our services.

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to collect this information (see Section 5 below).  

Information Related to Services: If you engage with our Services, we may collect information relevant to the specific services we are providing to you, which may include project details, technical specifications, and communication records.

We may use your personal information for the following purposes:

To Provide and Maintain Our Services: To operate our Website and provide you with the information and Services you request.   To Communicate with You: To respond to your inquiries, provide updates about our Services, and send you relevant information. To Improve Our Website and Services: To analyze how our Website and Services are used, identify trends, and make improvements. For Marketing Purposes: With your consent where required by applicable law, we may send you promotional emails or other marketing communications about our Services that may be of interest to you. You can opt-out of receiving these communications at any time (see Section 6 below).   For Legal and Compliance Purposes: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes. For Security Purposes: To protect our Website and Services from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure.

We may share your personal information with the following categories of recipients:

These service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the purposes for which we disclose it to them.   Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, reorganization, sale of assets, or similar transaction, your information may be transferred to a successor entity.

4. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information, including the right to:  

Access: Request access to the personal information we hold about you. Correction: Request that we correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal information. Deletion: Request that we delete your personal information under certain circumstances.   Restriction of Processing: Request that we restrict the processing of your personal information under certain circumstances.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies (e.g., pixels, web beacons) to collect information about your browsing activities on our Website. Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. We use cookies to:

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our Website.  

6. Your Choices Regarding Marketing Communications

If you no longer wish to receive marketing communications from us, you can opt-out by following the unsubscribe instructions included in our emails or by contacting us directly.

Therefore, while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.
